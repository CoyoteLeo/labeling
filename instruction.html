Background

- There are two articles and multiple pair that consist of two short paragraphs(most less than 3 sentences) from the article, named recalled paragraph and retold paragraph with events of the paragraphs.

Annotation

Steps
  1. Read the original articles
  2. Read the paragraphs
  3. Connect the events with correct label base on the information from articles and paragraphs.
  4. Switch to next pair until the enc, and submit.
Relation Label
Match: The event from both recalled paragraph and retold paragraph describe the same thing, and there are no conflicts between each other.(i.e. they can use different words, but express the same meaning.), there are some examples below
relation recalled event retold event reason
Match Our family, consisting of, 8 - year old son. we, have, an 8 year old different description, but same meaning
Match We, proceeded to, our hotel. We, reaching, our hotel on the Canadian side the second is more detailed, but both of them have same meaning.
Match 1. he, asked, me
2. he, asked, marry him 		he, propose Each recalled/retold event may matched to multiple retold/recalled events.
Conflict: In contrast to "Match", Conflict means the events describe the same thing, but the meaning is inconsistent. there are some examples below
relation recalled event retold event reason
Conflict No one, plays, sports you, do, sports Same event, but their meaning are conflict.
Conflict I, walk to, school I, drive to, school Same event, but their meaning are conflict.
(No relation): If there is not any relations between event, you can just leave it empty and continue to label the next pair
